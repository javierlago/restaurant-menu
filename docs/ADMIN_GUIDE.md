### Adding a Dish
- Click the **"+" (Add Dish)** button.

### Editing a Dish
- Click the **Edit (Pencil)** icon on any dish card.
- Modify the information and click **"Save"**.

### Hiding a Dish (Out of Stock)
- Click the **Eye** icon. 
- If the icon is slashed (**FaEyeSlash**), the dish is hidden from customers but remains in your dashboard for future use.
